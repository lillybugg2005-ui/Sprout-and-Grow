Upcoming Events & Workshops

Sprout & Grow Spring Festival

If you love springtime, this event is perfect for you! Join us for the Sprout & Grow Spring Festival — a fun, family‑friendly celebration of plants, learning, and community. Explore our Spring Plant Sale, where you can discover new plants and learn how to care for them from our team. Throughout the day, enter our Spring Raffle for a chance to win prizes like a Plant Buddy, a Plant Sensor, a UV Grow Light, and a Mystery Seed Bag. Kids are welcome too! Young plant lovers can decorate their own small pot and plant a seed to take home — learning how to care for their tiny sprout as it grows. Enjoy live music, local food trucks, and plenty of springtime activities for all ages. Come celebrate the season with us — one plant at a time.

Garden Glow Night

Step into the magic of Garden Glow Night — an enchanting evening filled with lights, plants, and peaceful springtime energy. As the sun sets, our garden transforms into a glowing wonderland with lanterns, string lights, and softly illuminated pathways. Explore our collection of night‑blooming plants, learn about evening pollinators, and enjoy guided mini‑tours through the glowing garden. Throughout the night, enter our Glow Raffle for a chance to win a Night Garden Starter Kit, a UV Grow Light, or a set of glow‑in‑the‑dark plant markers. Sip warm herbal tea, listen to calming live music, and take photos at our glowing plant backdrop. Join us for a magical night under the lights — where plants shine in a whole new way.

Fall Harvest Celebration

Celebrate the cozy side of the season at our Fall Harvest Celebration — a warm, autumn‑themed event filled with plants, crafts, and community fun. Browse our Fall Plant Market, featuring mums, ornamental peppers, fall herbs, and seasonal houseplants. Enter our Harvest Raffle for a chance to win a Fall Garden Bundle, a Pumpkin Planter Kit, or a mystery bag of autumn seeds. Kids can decorate their own mini pumpkin planter and plant a fall seed to take home. Enjoy warm cider, fall treats, and a leafy photo backdrop perfect for seasonal pictures. Come enjoy the colors, smells, and excitement of fall — one cozy plant at a time.

The Big Terrarium Build‑Off

Get ready for creativity, competition, and a whole lot of fun at the Big Terrarium Build‑Off — our most hands‑on event of the season! Participants receive a terrarium kit with soil, stones, moss, and a selection of mini plants. Build your own unique terrarium, decorate it, and submit it for judging in categories like Most Creative, Best Theme, and Prettiest Plant Arrangement. Winners will receive prizes such as a Plant Buddy, a Gift Card, or a Deluxe Terrarium Kit. Even if you’re not competing, you can watch the builds, learn terrarium tips from our team, and enjoy the excitement of the event. Join us for a day of creativity, friendly competition, and plant‑powered fun — and see what you can grow inside a glass world.

Join us for hands‑on classes, community gatherings, and seasonal workshops designed to help gardeners of all ages learn and grow.
