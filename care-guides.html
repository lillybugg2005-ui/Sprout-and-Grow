Plant Care Guides

Whether you're caring for your first houseplant or managing a full garden, these simple guides will help you keep your plants healthy and thriving.

Watering Basics

Most plants prefer consistent moisture but dislike sitting in water. Always check the top inch of soil before watering and ensure your pots have proper drainage.

Light Requirements

Understanding light levels is key to plant success. South-facing windows offer bright, direct light, while north-facing windows provide gentle, indirect light ideal for shade‑loving plants.

Soil & Fertilizer

Use high‑quality organic soil blends and feed your plants with natural fertilizers during the growing season for best results.
